Double pendula are known to be the most iconic example of chaotic motion as the constant changing of variables makes them very unpredictable. So I made one to test them out. This was my first intro to Processing so I followed a video by The Coding Train to get me started and fiddled around with that, following the equations similar to the ones on here. Colors included as well.
