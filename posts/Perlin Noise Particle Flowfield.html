After discovering Perlin Noise from the terrain generator, I decided to play around a bit more using a particle system and a grid of vectors that guide the dots along flowlines. The sketch is controlled through a overall fineness variable that determines vector size and runtime (should control particle count too but I didn't). See pictures for results with varying fineness levels.
